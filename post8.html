Amtrak for Cheap

Saving a lot of money

How can Students save money on Amtrak.

December 26, 2016 by Devesh

Tweet

This post is for students and people who use Amtrak for travelling.

Many people use Amtrak as there means of transport from from one city to another, mostly students or people who don't have a car. I have used Amtrak quite a few times and I feel it is quite expensive. I paid $40 for a ride from San Jose to Sacramento which is a lot if you compare it with other modes of public transportation or car pooling. Though it gives you a lot of benefits while travelling but still it's costly as compared to other services like Greyhound or Megabus.

But how can you travel Amtrak for cheap?

So for the same ticket which was $40 in Price, it was only 1380 points if you purchase through Amtrak Guest Rewards Points. It comes around 2.9 cents per point, which I think is pretty decent rate for these points to use.

Now how can you get the points

  1. So when you sign up for Amtrak Guest Rewards and take a ride you get a bonus of 500 points. Sign up here.
  2. There are few credit cards which offer a sign up bonus of 10,000 - 30,000 points and other points on purchase of Amtrak tickets.
  3. Amtrak also offers discounts for Students of 15%.

If you just get 30000 points as sign up bonus, you can do almost 22 one-way rides from San Jose to Sacramento or any other regular place you commute to, which is $880. Imagine the savings you can do if you use Amtrak for regular travelling.

I believe it is a very good deal to save money especially for students who can use this money for their tuition, books or other things.
