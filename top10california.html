Top 15 Places to visit in California.

California is often quoted as "The America of America". It boasts everything from the hottest place in the world (Death valley) to Lake Tahoe which sometimes goes to -20 degrees, from the tall Redwood forests to wide Sequioa trees in Sequioa National Park, from the ever beautiful Yoesimte Valley which is around 4000 years old to the Silicon Valley where everyday a new thing is invented which can change the world in its own ways. Welcome to the Dream, Welcome to California.
