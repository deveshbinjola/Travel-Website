Two ways to travel cheap are:

  1. Book with miles and points. In case if your wondering about miles and points, Check out my guide on earning miles and points. Few good websites for finding award travel are: AwardHacker.com, AwardAce.com
  2. The other way to find cheap fares is through Mistake Fares. Yes, mistakes fares do happen in this world and you can find it many times.

    3. What if you don't have miles and points and you don't even have enough time to earn them. Check out how to buy tickets. Check out how to book cheap tickets.

    The best website I like to search air fares is Kayak.com. It gives the easiness to book flights, is fast and almost gives me the best deals. The other websites I really like are Skyscanner, Booking.com.

    The best website I like to search hotels is Booking.com. It gives you a lot of options to choose from. The website is growing up quickly and is becoming better day by day. The other website I like is Priceline.com.

    The best place to rent cars is through the website of the rental companies. There are always many coupons
