How to waive the Young Driver Fee?

Waiving the extra fee.

Posted by The Travel Student on August 31, 2016
Tweet

Car rental companies charge drivers under age of 25 with a Young Driver Fee worth $15 - $25 per day. Most of the students are under 25 also many of th readers are under 25. It costs them a lot for a renting car for daily purposes or vacations. So how to avoid these useless fees?

The best way to avoid the Young Driver fee is by taking the USAA Membership. It is reserved for Military people and their families but it allows other people to take membership and it is free of cost as well for using certain products and Car rentals is one of them.

Taking USAA membership allows you (people under 25) save $15 - $25 per day. This is best way to save money on car rentals. You can save on Hertz, Enterprise, AVIS, Budget from USAA membership. Though i have only used it on Hertz and Enterprise. I have saved over $500 in this useless fee. I hope you guys can also take advantage to this membership and save money.   2. After you are all set, search for auto car rentals.
