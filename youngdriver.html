How to waive the Young Driver Fee

Waiving the extra fee.

Jully 22, 2016 by Devesh

Car rental companies charge drivers under age of 25 with a Young Driver Fee worth $15 - $25 per day. Most of the students are under 25 and it costs them a lot for renting card for daily purposes or vacations. So how to avoid these useless fees?

The best way to avoid the Young Driver fee is by taking the USAA Membership. It is reserved for Military people and their families but it allows other people to take membership.
