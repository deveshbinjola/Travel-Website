Google Express with Groupon

Saving a lot of money.

Groupon

July 22, 2016 by Devesh

Groupon has a deal going on in which they are selling $40 of Google Express coupon for $15. Google Express sells items from Costco, Staples, Kohls and many other brands to your home (in selected regions) within two days. Yes, there is a membership fee for it but the first 3 months are absolutely free.


Google Express Delivery Stores.

How to use it?

  1. Purchase the coupon from Groupon. Don't have Groupon account, Sign up for one.
  2. Before purchasing try putting codes like FIRST (for first time customers), SAVE20, EXTRA20, SUMMER20 and more to save more money.
  3. Get the Google Express promo code and copy it.
  4. Go to Google Express and sign up for new membership, keep the membership plan for $10/month instead of $95/year. Don't worry the first 3 months are fre.
  5. Choose the products you want to buy from your favorite available stores.
  6. Enter the promo code while checkout and enjoy your products for cheap.
