Using Airline Fee

Making money

How to use the airline credit fee to the full extent

November 27, 2016 by Devesh

Tweet

In this post I am going to tell you how to utilize that $100/$200 airline credit fee to its full extent and other perks offered with travel credit cards.

Cards that offer Airline Credit Fee

  • Premier Rewards Gold Card from American Express - $100
  • Platinum Card from American Express - $250

    • How can you use this airline credit fee.

    You can use also use this airline credit fee even though without sitting in a plane. You can buy gift cards from the airlines. You can also buy Amazon and Ebay gift cards from this airline credit fee and even from many other things. Here's how to redeem this:

  • Select United as your airline to redeem the airline credit fee.
  • Download the MileagePlusX app from United in your phone (I have checked for Android and iPhone).
  • Add your card to the payment option which has the airline credit fee to redeem.
  • Select Online merchants from menu button from left top corner.
  • Choose your merchant (Ebay, Amazon or any other thing you want to buy) and choose the denomination of the gift card ($25, $50 or $100). Go ahead with payment process.
  • I will advice you buy a $25 gift card first to check if its working. It takes 2-3 days to show up after your transaction has been posted.
  • You also United miles with this offer, though its too less but still Something is better than nothing.

    • I purchased $100 worth of Ebay gift cards and used them to buy an Iphone from eBay.

    How to maximize this airline credit fee?

    The airline credit fee is provided by the year, so it will be given once in 2016 and then in 2017. So you can get your card in December'2016 and use youe airline credit fee for 2016 in 2016 and then in 2017 you ahgain get it.

    So you can get $200 in airline credit fee for Premier Rewards Gold Card from American Express for 0 annual fee and $500 for Platinum Card for $450 of annual fee.

    You can also downgrade the card next year if ypu don't want the card. Check out the downgrade options.
