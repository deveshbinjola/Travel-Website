Reconsideration Phone Numbers For Credit Cards

What do you do when your application for a Credit Card is not accepted?

Posted by The Travel Student on March 8, 2019
Tweet

It happens so many times that you apply for a Credit Card and you are excited to get your new credit card but you get a rejection or you get a review status. So what do you do?
The best thing is to call the Credit card company's reconsideration line and ask them for a reason. Many times its a real small problem but we don't know about it. If it is something bigger try asking them how can you still get the card. Also try calling them again it they don't approve you on the first call. It really depends on the person you are talking to, some people tend to be good and some not so good.
