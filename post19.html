AMEX Offer: $5 off $5 Android Pay (upto 3x)
The Travel Student

Tweet

American Express is running an offer of $5 off $5 when you use your enrolled card in Android Pay. The best thing is that you can use it upto 3 times. So you can earn straight $15 from this deal. This deal is targeted and may not be available on all your American Express Cards. I got this deal for just one of my 4 American Express cards.


The all new AMEX Offer.

Redeeming the deal:

  • You need an Android Smartphone to redeem this deal as Android Pay is specific to Android smartphones.
  • You can redeem this deal in any store (almost every store accepts Android Pay).
  • You can also redeem it online on the stores that accept Android Pay online.
  • You can also redeem it on Uber and Lyft. If you use Uber and Lyft regularly this is the best deal for you.
  • Offer expires 5/3/17.

My Verdict:

I believe this is a great offer for American Express customers and people who use Android smartphone. It is an opportunity make free $15. Don't let it go.
