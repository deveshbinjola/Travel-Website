Capital One has launched a new credit card called “Premier Dining Rewards“. Here are the few features of the card.
Top features of the card:

  • 3% Cashback on Dining.
  • 2% Cashback on Groceries.
  • No Foriegn Transaction Fee.
  • $100 Signup Bonus.


The New Capital One card.

My Verdict:

I believe the card is good but not that great. I will only recommend this card to people who are looking for a card with no foriegn transaction fee and some decent cashback without paying any annual fee. If someone is looking for a Cashback card there are many better cards offering better cashback like Chase Freedom Unlimited (1.5x), USAA Limitless (2.5x) and many more.
