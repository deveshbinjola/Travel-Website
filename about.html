I am a traveller, photographer, travel enthusiast and travel hacker. By travel hacker I means I find ways to travel cheaper or for free. I love travelling and my goal is to help people with their credit and make their travelling easier. I came to United States when I was 17 and I did not knew anything about credit. Why did I start this Website?
I have been seeing students doing really bad in regards of credit. I want to help them to have a good credit history. People think it is hard and there is a lot to understand. I have simplified the process in few steps. You can't even imagine how much money you can actually save by it.
How do you know this stuff?
I have been reading many different travel blogs for past few years and have learnt a lot. I have tried to collabarate all that knowledge into this website. I feel that no one focuses much on students. As being a student, I want to help my peers.
Who can be benefited by this website?
Anyone. Yes frankly anyone. There are so many tips and tricks that can help anyone.
How can you help us?
There is no price for any material on this website, you can help us by using the links on this website. You can also help us by sharing your stories that how did this website helped you in any way. You can also share about your Trips or any fun fact or hack you have which can help others.
