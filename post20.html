Groupon has a deal going on in which they are selling $40 of Google Express coupon for $15. Google Express sells items from Costco, Staples, Google store, Kohls, Whole Foods and many other brands to your home within two days. Google Express is now available throughout the continental United States. Yes, there is a membership fee for it but the first 3 months are absolutely free.


How to use it?

  1. Purchase the coupon from Groupon. Don't have Groupon account, Sign up for one.
  2. Before purchasing try putting codes like WELCOME (for first time customers) to save more money. You can search for more coupons online.
  3. Get the Google Express promo code after you redeem the groupon and copy it.
  4. Go to Google Express and sign up for new membership using your google account, keep the membership plan for $10/month instead of $95/year. Don't worry the first 3 months are free.
  5. Choose the products you want to buy from your favorite available stores.
  6. Enter the promo code while checkout and enjoy your products for cheap.
  7. Don't forget to cancel Google Exress membership after the delivery, or you can keep until the three month free period.

3 months of free delivery using Google Express(select locations).

Things to keep in mind.

  1. You have to be a first time google express user to use the the coupon, you can make many different google accounts and then use it.
  2. Don't use it for unnecessary stuff.
  3. This is a amazing deal for Costco lovers.

Making more money with it

  • Order it multiple times and redeem it with different google accounts, making google accounts are free.
  • You can also refer yourself on groupon to earn $10 Groupon bucks per referral.

A similar deal came last year where I ordered around 10 times using this deal which cost me less than $70, thus saving me around $330. It is a really great deal to save money and google express is a really good website to order your essentials and other stuff from stores like Costco and others.
