Redeeming Miles and Points

How to redeem Miles and Points?

July 22, 2016 by Devesh

There are 4 major credit card rewards program.

Chase Ultimate Rewards

You can transfer these points to 7 different airlines and 4 different hotel chains. These points can also be transferred to Amazon for shopping. You can also get these points as cashback but transferring them seems to be the best option.

Chase Ultimate Rewards Partners.

American Express Membership Rewards.

Membership Rewards which can be transferred to 17 different airlines and 3 hotel chains. These points can also be transferred to Amazon for shopping and Uber for rides. You can also pay your credit card bills using the points but that is not a good option to use points.

Membership Rewards Partners.

Starwood Guest Rewards.

We can transfer Starpoints to over 30 airline frequent flyer programs, mostly on a one-to-one basis. Receive a 5,000 Starpoint bonus when you transfer 20,000 Starpoints to a participating frequent flyer program.

Starwood Preferred Guest Partners.

Citi Thank You Points.

It transfer points 12 airlines and 1 hotel chain. These points can also be transferred to Amazon for shopping. These points can also be transferred to Amazon for shopping.

Citi Thank You Partners.
