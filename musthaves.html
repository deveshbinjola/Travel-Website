The Travel Essentials

Must Haves

The Ultimate Travel Packing List.

January 22, 2017 by Devesh

Tweet
  1. Google Trip App

    2. I highly recommend every traveller to keep this app on his/her phone. This app stores all your reservations and itineraries automatically from you email. This app also allows you to download their nearby guide of the place you are travelling. This app is my #1 Must have for travellers.

  2. Eye Mask and Ear Plugs

    3. Whether you sleep on a plane or anywhere, whether you sleep light or deep, this will help somewhere or another. You can buy these from Amazon or any other cheap retail websites.

  3. Headphones/Earpods

    4. Theses are really necessary if you want to listen music or watch videos/movies. You can also buy Noise Cancellation headphones if you want to sleep in peace.

  4. Hand Sanitizer/ Wet wipes

    5. This is really helpful in trips even if you health consious or not.

  5. Check Important Travel Documents, Cash, and Credit Cards

    6. Make sure you keep your Personal ID, Passport, Credit Cards, Cash. I usually recommend not to use cash anywhere but if you are travelling abroad you should keep some cash with you, in order to exchange money. You might need cash in many South American and South Asian countries. Keep cash with you all the times but use it when there is no other option available.

  6. Portable Powerbank Charger

    7. I recommend keeping a portable powerbank charger, it can help you if you are low in battery.

  7. Google Maps Offline

    8. You can download offline maps on Google maps. So whenever you travelling and you want save data or even if you don't have data, this app help you with directions without using data.

  8. Water Bottle

    9. This might sound silly but water bottle will help you anywhere while you are travelling.

  9. Ziplocks

    10. These are handy in carrying stuff.

  10. Performance/KO Hoodies/Sweatshirts

    11. These are really helpful if you are hiking or even just going to a cool place. I always wear these while hiking.

  11. Universal Adapter.

    12. Really great for people travelling abroad.
