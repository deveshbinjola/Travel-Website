WAYS TO EARN POINTS FOR CHEAPER TRAVEL.

  1. Credit Card Sign Up Bonuses

    2. You can earn the major portion of your reward points through credit card bonuses which range from 10,000 Points to 100,000 Points.

    Chase Sapphire Preferred gives 50,000 as sign up bonus when you spend $4,000 in first 3 months.

  2. Credit Card Rewards

    3. Credit card rewards means the points you earn by spending through your credit cards. Every card ofers different type of reward points.

    Premier Gold Rewards card from American Express earns 3 points per $1 on tickets purchased directly from airlines, 2 points per $1 at restaurants, gas station and supermarkets and 1 point per $1 spent everywhere else.

  3. Earn Rewards from Banking

    4. You can earn points and miles or cash for opening a new checking or saving acoount. There are some debit cards which offers points or miles on transactions.

OTHER WAYS FOR CHEAPER TRAVEL.

  1. Mistake Fares

    2. Mistakes Fares happen more often than not. You just have to be lucky to know about it at the right time. Last year a President Suite in Grand Hyatt, Santiago, Chile worth more than $1000 a night was available $5 per night just because of a mistake fare. Ther are many more stories like that where super expensive airline or hotel tickets were available for super cheap. You just need to subscribe to my newsletter or follow us on Twitter to keep in touch with all these offers.

  2. Bonus Free Nights

    3. Many Credit Cards like Citi Prestige offers 4th night free on reservation for 4 or more nights which saves a lot of money if you are frequent traveller.

  3. Stopovers

    4. Stopovers are really helpful if you travel cheaply and it is one of the most important trick to redeem points. United offers one stopover and two open jaws with every round trip.> You can check more about stopovers here.

  4. Checking fo better seats or getting bumped off the flight

    5. You should always check with airline staff or hotel staff about a better seat o room. I have been upgraded to Business class ona Etihad Flight from Abu Dhabi to San Francisco just for asking them a better seat.

    You can also volunteer to get bumped off the flight to earn big rewards from airlines. It works when certain flights, routes and airlines often oversell flights and some people are okay to over pay to get in the flight. Bumping off can help you earn many rewards.

Conclusion

These are some simple tips and tricks to cheaper you travel using credit cards and other stuff. You just need to use the right credit cards at right plaves and through right places to earn huge number rewards. I would recommend all my readers or anyone else who are using credit cards to pay their balance in full every month. Credit card companies have a lot of offers and they compensate these offers by charging a hefty APR (generally around 12% - 20%). Also paying your credit cards in full every month keeps your credit score healthy so I will urge everyone to pay their credit cards in full every month.
