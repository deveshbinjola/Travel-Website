Credit Cards

Which one to choose?

Different types of Credit Cards.

July 22, 2016 by Devesh

Tweet

A credit card allows us to make a purchase and allows us to pay over time. Then there are more things to it APR, Balance Transfers and all. But we are not dealing with all this. The most important thing using a credit card is to pay the balance in full every month; if you can't do I won't suggest to use credit cards. We try to use credit cards for other benefits that people doesn't even know about. Here is a list of different credit cards od different types and I explain the benefits, try applying foe ones that best suits your requirements and needs. One thing I suggest people is to go for credit cards with points instead of cashback, I'll explain why when you read further. The 4 different types of credit cards are:

  1. Airline Credit Card

    2. Airline Credit Cards are offered from Airlines with credit card issuers. These cards offer a signup bonus ranging from 10,000 to 100,000 miles. These miles can only be used for the airline the card is issued for. The card also offers extra benefits like:

    1. Free Check-in baggage for upto a family of 4.
    2. Priority Boarding (Faster boarding).
    3. Some airlines like Alaska Airlines and Southwest offer a companion pass.
    4. These cards also offer VIP Status (Gold, Silver, etc.) for the Airlines loyalty programs which have many other benefits.
    5. You can check my list of miles valuations here. This might give you heads up which ones are the best for you.
    6. Most of the cards also offer No Foriegn Transaction Fee.
    7. There is a annual fee for most of the cards but check out my post to see if that is worth it or not.

  2. Cashback Credit Card

    3. Cashback Cards are offered from credit card issuers which offer cashback on different categories ranging from 1% to 5%. These cards are good for people who don't like to travel and want to have some cash from their credit cards. I don't feel like that cashback cards are of ay good use though they good cashback deals. But there are some of the cashback credit cards which can be combined with other points credit cards which make a whole lot of sense.

  3. Hotel Points

    4. Hotel Credit Cards are offered from Hotels with credit card issuers. These cards offer a signup bonus ranging from 10,000 to 100,000 miles. These points can only be used for the hotel chains the card is issued for. The card also offers extra benefits like:

    1. Early Check-ins and Late Check-outs.
    2. Many cards like IHG, Mariott, Ritz Carlton and more offer a free night every year.
    3. These cards also offer VIP Status (Gold, Platinum, Diamond, etc.) for the Hotel loyalty programs which have many other benefits. Check out my review of Hilton Diamond Status.
    4. You can check my list of hotel points valuations here. This might give you heads up which ones are the best for you.
    5. Most of the cards also offer No Foriegn Transaction Fee.
    6. There is a annual fee for most of the cards but most of these cards offer a free night which mostly justifies the annual fee but many cards offer great sign up bonuses without any annual fee.

  4. Other Points

    5. These are easily the best type of credit cards for any who wants to travel at a cheaper price. These cards offer a signup bonus ranging from 30,000 to 100,000 miles. The card also offers extra benefits like:

    1. These points can be used for different hotels and airlines according to the transfer partners of the credit card issuers.
    2. There are many options to use your points as airline miles and hotel points.
    3. You can also use points on Amazon, paying your credit card and many more things. But using it as miles is the best option.
    4. You can check my list of points valuations here. This might give you heads up which ones are the best for you.
    5. Almost all the cards also offer No Foriegn Transaction Fee.

    Four main types of rewards programs:
    1. Chase Ultimate Rewards.
    2. American Express Membership Rewards.
    3. Starwood Guest Rewards.
    4. Citi Thank You Points.
