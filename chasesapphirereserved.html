Chase Sapphire Reserved

Chase has announced Chase Sapphire Reserved with 100,000 bonus Ultimate Reward Points. The term s and conditions for the bonus ar not confirmed yet but I thing it should be around spending $5,000 - $7,000 in first 3 months.

The brand new Chase Sapphire Reserve Card.

The card will earn 3x points on all travel and dining purchases which is an upgrade for 2x points for Chase Sapphire Preferred. It also offers a $300 annual travel credit which can be applied to any expenses that is coded as travel, including hotels, airfare, taxis, trains and any other charges related to travel which is really great if compared to any other high end card. It also offers full Priority Pass Select membership, giving you access to more than 900 airport lounges around the world. It also offers $100 Global entry fee.

Being a Visa Infinite card, it has several others too. It has a $450 annual fee but I believe it is okay as compared to its benefits. The best benefit is the $300 travel credit for any travel purchase.


Should you apply for for his card?

I will say if you are someone who travel once in a month or more, you should go for this card. If you don not travel much, I would like you to wait for my next post when the card comes in my wallet.
